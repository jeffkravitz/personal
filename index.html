Welcome to Jeff's page!

I am a social psychologist currently working
in the Emotion and Morality Lab at Brooklyn College. I earned my M.A. in Experimental Psychology from Brooklyn College and my B.S. in Psychology from Stony Brook University. My research has focused on a) how social-evolutionary perspectives can explain the relationship between moral judgment and emotion, b) whether perceptions of stress can cause harsher moral judgment, and c) if mindfulness meditation can reduce inherent bias in decision making.

My ultimate career goal is to become a professor of psychology at a research university and continue my work in moral psychology. Along the way, I plan to complete a PhD in social psychology. I am currently looking for a job as a research assistant or lab manager in a lab that studies morality, social cooperation, social learning, or emotion.
